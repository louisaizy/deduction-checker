Deduction Checker

Use "*" for •, "=>" for ⊃, "<=>" for ≡, "UQ" for ∀, "EQ" for Ǝ and "!=" for ≠.

Please put all universally or existentially quantified schemata in parentheses.

For example, you might write: (UQx)(x=x).
